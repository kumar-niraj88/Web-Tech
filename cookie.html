Chocolate chip Cookies

Prep time :15 minutes

Cook time : 10 minutes

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350oF (180oF)
  2. Mix flour and suger in a bowl
  3. add chocolate chips and stir weel
  4. bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown

Ingredient

Ingrident Quantity
flour 2 cups
Suger 1 cups
chocolate chips 1 cups

Enjoy your homemade cookie!
